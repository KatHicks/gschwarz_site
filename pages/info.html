Gabrielle Schwarz is a writer, curator and editor based in London.

She studied English at the University of Cambridge before deciding to move into the visual arts. She now holds an MA in Curating the Art Museum from the Courtauld Institute of Art (London) and has worked on exhibitions at the Courtauld Gallery, the Institute of Contemporary Arts London and Hales Gallery.

Schwarz writes for various publications including Another Gaze, the ICA Blog and this is tomorrow, and is a regular contributor to Apollo magazine.

Some (but by no means all) of her favourite topics include: installation art, social engagement in the arts, photography, sound and moving image, archives, questions of authorship and interpretation, selfies and the colour pink.
